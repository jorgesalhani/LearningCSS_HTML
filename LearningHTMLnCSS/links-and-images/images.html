Images

This page covers common image formats, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos, especially with with gradient colors.

GIFs

GIFs actually is read like jif.

PNGs

PNG images do not have color palette limitations, hence they are excellent fit for icons, logos, etc.

SVGs

SVG images are vector-based, thus perfect for scale up or down without loss of quality. Their default dimensions are 75x75 pixels. The others use 150x150 pixels.
